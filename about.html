About

My name is Valeria Di Niscia, a 21 years old graphic designer and illustrator. Passionate about typography, editorial design, web design and illustration.

I have +4 years of experience working in the field of design with brands from Spain, Chile, Peru, Argentina, United States and Mexico.

Some of them are Victoria’s Secret, Bath & Body Works, Aeropostale, MilSabores Holding (Astrid y Gastón, La Mar, Tanta, La Sanguchera del Barrio, El Japonés, Barra Chalaca, Take a Wok), Tavelli Coffee Shops, Le Fournil Restaurants, Sangú Restaurants, among others.

My dog Ada is half dalmatian, half wolf, 100% cute. Love long walks and its puffy duck toy "Mr. Ducky"

I love reading books, watching movies and anime, exploring new cities, and playing with my dog. A firm believer that books should be judged by their cover, after all, someone designed them, right?
